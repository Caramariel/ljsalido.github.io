Importance Of Fire Extinguisher

Fire extinguishers are essential components of any fire safety plan in any environment. They are a first-aid response to a fire and can help prevent major property damage and even death. If you operate a business or a building, you are responsible for providing suitable firefighting equipment on the premises, and this legislation requires you to maintain and conduct regular inspections of the fire extinguishers.


PULL

Pull the pin on the fire extinguisher.


AIM

Aim the extinguisher nozzle on the hose low, toward the base of the fire.


SQUEEZE

Squeeze the handle to discharge the extinguisher


SWEEP

Sweep the nozzle back and forth. Keep the fire extinguisher aimed at the base of the fire and move it from side to side until the flames are extinguished.
