FIRE AT HOME

WHAT TO DO BEFORE

Escape Plans

⚪Establish an evacuation plan with multiple escape routes.

⚪Assign a location to meet outside the establishment

Alarm System and Safety Equipment

⚪Install smoke alarms in bedrooms and nearby areas.

⚪Store and learn how to use fire extinguishers at home.

Guard Against Kitchen Fires

⚪Never leave cooking food unattended.

⚪Seal the LPG tank when not in use.

Candle And Incense

⚪Make sure to blow out active candles and incense.

⚪Keep candles away from inflammable materials.

Electrical Safety

⚪Replace or fix any frayed, loose or damaged cords.

⚪Avoid using electrical appliances near wet places.

Inflammable Substances

⚪Keep inflammable materials out of reach of children.

⚪Keep flammable liquids in a dry and cool location.

WHAT TO DO DURING

DO NOT PANIC

⚪Remain calm and focus on what to do and how to get out of the fire immediately.

⚪Yell "Fire!" several times and go outside right away.

⚪Stay low to avoid smoke, fumes, and superheated gasses that may have entered.

⚪Act immediately and stay calm.

⚪Don’t waste time getting dressed or searching for valuables.

EVACUATE IMMEDIATELY

⚪Cover your mouth and face as you go out.

⚪Never use elevators, instead use the stairs.

⚪Maintain an accessible exit.

⚪Move quickly to an open area.

⚪If your clothing catches fire, stop, drop and roll.

EXTINGUISH

⚪Call for help.

⚪Immediately contact the local fire department (911).

⚪Use fire extinguishers.

⚪Report all fires to a supervisor.

⚪Alert people in the area.

PAY ATTENTION

⚪Always be alert of your surroundings.

⚪Pay attention to the announcement of the local fire department .

⚪Continue to check in with news update for information about the fire .

⚪Don’t open the door if you see smoke coming under the door.

⚪Check to see if there's heat or smoke coming in the cracks around the door .

WHAT TO DO AFTER

DO'S

⚪Follow fire department advice and recommendations.

⚪Inform your relatives and friends about your status.

⚪Photograph the incident as proof of absence from schoolwork.

⚪Throw away any consumables that have been damaged by the fire.

⚪Preserve any furniture that can be restored or refurbished.

⚪Look for critical papers that were not destroyed by the fire.

⚪Make a list of everything that is damaged in the building.

⚪Turn off services such as gas, water, and electricity until it is confirmed that it is safe to do so.

DON'TS

⚪Entering the premises after the fire has been extinguished.

⚪Usage of electronic appliances.

⚪Taking refuge in a burned-out structure.

⚪Attempt to repair infrastructure damage without the aid of professionals.

⚪Consumption of charred food or water polluted by the fire.

⚪Reusing burnt-out fabrics.
